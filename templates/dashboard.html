Summary: SF Restaurant Health Scores

The Health Department has developed an inspection report and scoring system. After conducting an inspection of the facility, the Health Inspector calculates a score based on the violations observed. Violations can fall into: high risk category, moderate risk category, and low risk category. The score card that will be issued by the inspector is maintained at the food establishment and is available to the public in this dataset.

We analyzed the dataset by constructing the risk categories by restaurant, what year had the most high and low risk categories. We made multiple charts and maps to summarize our findings.
